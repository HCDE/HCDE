## Maintainers

CVAR reference pages are generated from the engine tree with `tools/generate-cvars-doc.ps1` (output: `wiki/CVAR-Reference.md`). Publish updates to GitHub Wiki with `tools/publish-hcde-wiki.ps1` after merging changes.
